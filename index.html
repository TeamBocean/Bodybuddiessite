Welcome To BodyBuddies

Body Buddies is a premier personal and small group training studio in Dublin 2, backed by professionals with over 50 years of fitness industry experience. We prioritize personal training excellence, nutrition guidance, and member results. Our top-class facilities and dedicated coaches provide effective training systems and knowledge foundations, ensuring members surpass their fitness goals. We champion real results without quick fixes, promising an outcome of enhanced energy, health, and strength.
